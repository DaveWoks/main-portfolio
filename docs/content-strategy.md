# Portfolio Content Strategy

## Purpose

Define the messaging, narrative, and communication strategy for the portfolio.

The goal is to ensure every section supports positioning, credibility, and conversion.

---

# Core Narrative

The portfolio is not about technology.

The portfolio is about solving problems.

Visitors should understand:

* What I do
* How I think
* What I have accomplished
* Why I am trustworthy
* Why I am different

---

# Primary Positioning Statement

The portfolio should communicate:

> I help teams and businesses transform complex ideas into thoughtful digital experiences through systems thinking, product thinking, design, and AI assisted development.

---

# Secondary Positioning

The portfolio owner operates at the intersection of:

* Design
* Engineering
* Product
* Leadership

---

# Audience Strategy

Different audiences care about different outcomes.

The portfolio adapts messaging while maintaining a single experience.

# Success Criteria

After reading the portfolio, visitors should understand:

Who I am.

What I do.

How I think.

What I have achieved.

Why I am credible.

How to contact me.

If those six questions are answered clearly, the content strategy is successful.
