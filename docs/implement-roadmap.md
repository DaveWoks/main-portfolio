# Development Rules

Do not skip phases.

Do not build animations before structure.

Do not build visuals before content.

Do not optimize before implementation.

Build from foundation upward.

---

# Definition of Success

Visitors should leave understanding:

* Who I am
* What I do
* How I think
* What I have built
* Why I am credible
* How to contact me

The portfolio should communicate expertise through clarity, structure, and execution.
