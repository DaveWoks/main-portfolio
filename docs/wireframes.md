# Portfolio Wireframe Architecture

## Purpose

This document defines the structure, layout, content hierarchy, and user journey of the portfolio before visual design and implementation.

The goal is to create a premium experience that communicates:

* Systems Thinking
* Frontend Architecture
* Product Thinking
* Leadership
* Business Impact

---

# User Journey

Visitors should naturally move through:

Who is this person?

↓

What does this person do?

↓

Can this person solve my problem?

↓

Can I trust this person?

↓

How do I contact this person?

---

* Strong typography

# Audience Selector

## Goal

Allow visitors to choose the perspective most relevant to them.

## Options

Recruiters

Engineers

Clients

Businesses

---

## Interaction

Selecting an audience mode updates:

* Hero copy
* Process copy
* Systems Thinking copy
* Experience emphasis

The rest of the site remains stable.

# Mobile Experience

Every section must work mobile first.

Rules:

* Single column layout
* Comfortable spacing
* Large touch targets
* Maintain hierarchy

---

# Interaction Rules

Everything moves.

Nothing dances.

Use:

* Fade
* Reveal
* Slide
* Stagger

Avoid:

* Bounce
* Spin
* Excessive effects

---

# Success Criteria

A visitor should leave knowing:

* Who I am
* What I do
* How I think
* What I have built
* Why I am credible
* How to contact me
